The (positive) applications of computing in entertainment

Accessibility and Inclusion

Computing in entertainment has led to the creation of tools and features that make content more accessible.

  • Closed captioning for movies, TV shows, and videos
  • Audiobooks and e-readers for visual impairments and learning disabilities
  • Customizable controls and adaptive technologies in video games

Creativity and Innovation

Computers have greatly improved the possible quality of media, especially with the use of GCI in movies and editing.

  • CGI and advanced graphics: Revolutionizing visual storytelling. Films like "Avatar" used motion capture to bring characters to life, while video games utilize high-resolution graphics for immersive experiences.
  • Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR): Creating new dimensions for interaction. VR headsets like Oculus Rift enable fully immersive virtual worlds, while AR apps like Pok√©mon GO blend digital elements with the physical world.

Short form content

Platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts have greatly benefited society. They've:

  • Provided greater access to online media for a wider audience, especially in developing countries.
  • Reduced the barriers for uploading content, making it more accessible.
  • Condensed information/media into more digestible chunks, more suitable for younger audiences.
